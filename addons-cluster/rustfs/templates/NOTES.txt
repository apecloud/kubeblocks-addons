RustFS cluster has been created successfully.

To access the RustFS S3 API:
  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "kblib.clusterName" . }}-rustfs 9000:9000

To access the RustFS Console:
  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "kblib.clusterName" . }}-rustfs 9001:9001

Get your credentials:
  export RUSTFS_ACCESS_KEY=$(kubectl get secret {{ include "kblib.clusterName" . }}-rustfs-account-root -o jsonpath='{.data.username}' | base64 -d)
  export RUSTFS_SECRET_KEY=$(kubectl get secret {{ include "kblib.clusterName" . }}-rustfs-account-root -o jsonpath='{.data.password}' | base64 -d)
